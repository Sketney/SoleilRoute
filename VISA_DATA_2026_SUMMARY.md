## Important Notes

### General Requirements
Most visa-free entries require:
- Passport valid for 6+ months (some countries)
- Proof of onward/return travel
- Proof of sufficient funds
- No intent to work (tourism/business only)

### ESTA (USA)
- Required for Visa Waiver Program countries
- Apply at least 72 hours before departure
- Valid for 2 years or until passport expires
- $21 fee

### eTA (Canada)
- Required for visa-exempt nationalities (except USA)
- Apply online, usually instant approval
- Valid for 5 years
- $7 CAD fee

### Schengen Area (90/180 Rule)
- Maximum 90 days within any 180-day period
- Applies to entire Schengen zone
- ETIAS coming in 2026 for many nationalities

### e-Visas
- Apply online before travel
- Processing typically 2-4 business days
- Print confirmation required
- Specific entry points only

## Recommendations for Users

1. **Always verify** current requirements at official embassy websites
2. **Apply early** - processing times can vary
3. **Check passport validity** - 6 months is common requirement
4. **Save copies** - keep digital/physical copies of all documents
5. **Travel insurance** - recommended for international travel
6. **COVID-19** - check current health requirements (as of Jan 2026, most restrictions lifted)
